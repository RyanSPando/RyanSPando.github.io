About Me

I am a web developer specializing in the JavaScript MEAN stack(Mongo/SQL, Express, AngularJS, and Node.js). The first decade of my career was in the material science field where I worked in assistant research positions or manufacturing teams. I have now decided to turn a love of computers into a career by attending Galvanize Full Stack Immersive program to hone my programming ability.

Throughout the Galvanize curriculum I have grown much more experienced with test driven development, Agile methodologies, pair programming, and Git/Github work flow on group projects.

I spend my down time with my two wonderful children, hiking Rocky Mountain National Park, and baking.
